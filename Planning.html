Planning!

STEP ONE
You will visit this link to see Dover Island which is sometimes called Mars on Earth because it resembles the Martian landscape.
STEP TWO
You will be designing four zones for the Martian Colony:

  • Food & Water
  • Energy
  • Fun & Exercise
  • Living Quarters
Each zone can have multiple buildings and will need to be realistic in terms of the technological capabilities of humanity. You need to use existing technology, emerging technology, or hypothetical technology that is currently in a protoype phase. The zones will need to be a closed system because the air on Mars would be toxic to people. Each zone should be connected to one another so people can travel back and forth between zones without having to go outside.
