Welcome to the Mars Colony Project Team. We need your help! An entrepreneur, Mr. Tusk, wants your help in planning a colony on Mars for 25 people, and if it works, it will be expanded. But first, we need your awesome ideas. Mr. Tusk is concerned about survival first and foremost (food, shelter, combating the dangerous environment), but he has also challenged us to make a colony that remembers the 25 pioneer colonists are also PEOPLE. People need fun things to do, so your plans should include spaces for fun and entertainment.
So, what do you need to do first? Watch the video below. After, you will start exploring the Martian terrain by first reading "What is a Rover?" from the dropdown menu above. Then, you will be looking at the latest Rover photos, and photos selected from 1000, 2000, and 3000 days from the rover's landing.
